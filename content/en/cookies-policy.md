## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. Similar technologies (local storage, pixels) may also be used. On this static site, cookie use is **minimal**.

## Cookies on this website

This Website does **not** use cookies for login, shopping carts, contact forms, or newsletter subscriptions — none of those features exist here.

We may use **third-party analytics cookies** through Google Analytics to understand aggregate visit statistics (pages viewed, general traffic patterns). These cookies are managed by Google.

We do **not** use advertising or profiling cookies on this Website.

## Third-party cookies

| Provider | Purpose | More information |
|----------|---------|------------------|
| Google Analytics | Anonymous or pseudonymous visit statistics | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

## How to control cookies

You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Instructions for common browsers:

* [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/clear-cookies-and-site-data-firefox)
* [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
* [Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge)
* [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac)

To opt out of Google Analytics specifically: [Google Analytics opt-out add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
